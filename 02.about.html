About Us

Welcome to RETROSONIC! We are a specialized shop dedicated to restoring and refurbishing vintage music players, including gramophones and jukeboxes. With a passion for preserving the nostalgic charm of these iconic devices, we carefully revive and bring them back to life, ensuring they retain their original character and functionality. At RETROSONIC, we understand the value of music history and the unique experience that vintage music players offer. Our skilled team of technicians meticulously works on each device, carefully restoring their mechanisms, replacing worn-out parts, and ensuring optimal performance. We pay great attention to detail, maintaining the authentic aesthetics while incorporating modern enhancements when necessary. Whether you are a collector, enthusiast, or simply looking to add a touch of nostalgia to your space, our collection of restored gramophones and jukeboxes will captivate you. Each piece tells its own story, preserving the essence of a bygone era and allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich sounds and memories they evoke. At RETROSONIC, we take pride in providing top-notch customer service. Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about vintage music players and is ready to assist you in finding the perfect piece for your collection or space. We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction and delivering a memorable experience that goes beyond the purchase. Discover the magic of vintage music players with RETROSONIC. Step into our shop and let the timeless melodies and exquisite craftsmanship transport you to a golden era of music.
